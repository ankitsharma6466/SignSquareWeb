The Company

As, Events and Signages have increasingly become an inseparable part of marketing, branding or sales exercise, from high-strung corporate houses to the neighborhood stores, everyone recognises the need of effective and powerful visual messages possible only through events and visual signages. Sign Square is leading in providing the services for Exhibitions, Events, Advertising and design media for indoor-outdoor Signage / Sign solutions and brand management, comprising of in-house Solvent, Digital Flex / Vinyl Printing, print and cut, and have experts for ACP Cladding, LED signages and all type of stage / backdrop fabrication.

The philosophy of Sign Square has always been to excel in what one knows best. In the process of development Sign Square has laid great emphasis on adopting latest technology & sincere efforts to improve quality by using latest tools, statistical techniques and procedures for all activities. The quality policy of Sign Square is to timely deliver quality products resulting into total customer satisfaction.

Every project at Sign Square, is dealt carefully ensuring the schedule, deadline and as per the quality requirements of our esteemed client.
